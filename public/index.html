Important: Scanned PDF Prerequisite

For scanned/image-based PDFs, please convert to TXT using PDF2Go OCR first. Modern text-based PDFs work directly.

Why is this needed?

Vercel serverless functions have CPU/memory limits that don't support OCR libraries like Tesseract. External OCR preprocessing allows us to deploy on free infrastructure while maintaining quality results. Most modern financial reports are text-based PDFs and work without conversion.
