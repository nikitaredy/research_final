TechCorp Q3 2025 Earnings Call Transcript
November 15, 2025

CEO Sarah Johnson:
Thank you for joining us today. I'm pleased to report that Q3 was another outstanding quarter for TechCorp. We delivered record revenue of $842 million, representing 47% year-over-year growth. This exceeded our guidance range of $800-820 million.

Our gross margin expanded to 68%, up from 64% in the prior year quarter, driven by favorable product mix and operational efficiencies. We're seeing tremendous momentum across all our product lines, particularly in our enterprise AI solutions which grew 89% year-over-year.

Looking ahead, we're raising our full-year revenue guidance to $3.1-3.2 billion, up from our previous range of $2.9-3.0 billion. For Q4, we expect revenue in the range of $900-950 million and operating margin of 22-25%.

I'm incredibly optimistic about our position in the market. We've secured partnerships with three Fortune 50 companies this quarter, and our customer retention rate stands at 98%, the highest in our company's history.

CFO Michael Chen:
From a financial perspective, we're in an excellent position. Operating cash flow reached $215 million in Q3, and we ended the quarter with $1.8 billion in cash and equivalents.

Currently, our data centers are operating at approximately 78% capacity, and we're planning to add 50% more capacity by mid-2026.

Our software subscription revenue grew to represent 72% of total revenue, up from 68% last quarter. This recurring revenue model provides strong visibility and predictability for our business.

However, I want to be transparent about some headwinds we're monitoring. We're seeing increased pricing pressure in the SMB segment due to new competitive entrants. Additionally, foreign exchange headwinds cost us approximately $12 million in Q3, and we expect this to continue into Q4.
